Website Description

Welcome to Mathias' Anniversary gift, a personal website dedicated to documenting and celebrating the milestones and memories of Mathias and Maria's journey together. Here, you'll find a collection of significant moments, captured in words, images and games, from their adventures in the first year of being a couple.
